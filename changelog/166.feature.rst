GenericSpectrogram.slice() method
---------------------------------

Added a `slice()` method to `GenericSpectrogram` that allows users
to easily extract a subset of the spectrogram by time and/or frequency.
The method updates metadata automatically and preserves the original
spectrogram.

- Use like: ``spec.slice(time=(t_start, t_end), freq=(f_min, f_max))``
- Fixes issue `#166`
